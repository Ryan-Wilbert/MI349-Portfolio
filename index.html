Welcome.

Hi there! I'm Ryan Wilbert, a student at Michigan State University studying Information Science. I am a passionate artist and researcher (currently) interested in how we can best use technology to handle the spread of information in society.

I come from Lake Orion, Michigan, and I have lived in the state for my whole life. The Midwest has influenced most of it — from my music taste to my general personality.

My hobbies include drawing, reading, writing, and creating music with my two guitars. I like to consider myself a hard working person, and with a quick browse of my work, I hope you can conclude the same.

I make stuff.

On this site, you’ll find a catalog of everything I’ve made. I'm always looking for new ways to challenge myself with a plethora of creative projects, ranging from screenplays to editorial pieces. Navigate between the pages above to find all of my work.
